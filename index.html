About Me

Research-Oriented Academic & Educator

I am a motivated and research-oriented academic with a Ph.D. in English from Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences. With extensive experience in higher education teaching and scholarly research, I am committed to fostering critical thinking, effective communication, and interdisciplinary learning through innovative and learner-centered pedagogical practices.

Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at Kristu Jayanti College (Deemed University), I am dedicated to contributing to academic excellence, research development, and the advancement of language and literary studies in higher education.
